Crispy Hash Brown Breakfast Casserole
with Bacon + Sausage + Carrots & Peas

Built like a tiny potato fortress: thaw, squeeze, pre-crisp. The eggs arrive later, politely, without turning everything into soggy folklore.

Tip Uncovered bake = less steam = more crisp

Ingredients

Scaled for 5. Cheese is optional if you’ve got it lurking in the fridge.
Prep: ~30 min Bake: ~45 min Total: ~75–85 min

Base

  • Frozen shredded hash browns: 1 bag (20–24 oz)
  • Eggs: 10
  • Bacon: 8–10 slices
  • Ground breakfast sausage: 8–12 oz
  • Frozen carrots + peas mix: 1 to 1½ cups
  • Optional: shredded cheese 1–1½ cups (cheddar/pepper jack)

Seasoning + Crisping

  • Oil / melted butter / bacon fat: 2–3 tbsp (for crisping hash browns)
  • Kosher salt: ~1½ tsp total (adjust for salty bacon)
  • Black pepper: 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder: 1 tsp
  • Smoked paprika: 1 tsp
  • Optional: pinch cayenne or red pepper flakes
The anti-soggy spell: thaw → squeeze dry → pre-crisp in the pan → cook veg dry → bake uncovered → rest before slicing.

Steps

  1. Heat oven + prep dish
    Heat to 425°F. Grease a 9x13 dish (wider dish = more crunchy edges).
    ~5 min
  2. Thaw + squeeze hash browns
    Microwave hash browns 2–3 min, stir, then 2–3 min more until thawed. Dump into a towel and squeeze until dry.
    ~10 min
  3. Pre-crisp the hash browns
    Toss dried hash browns with 2–3 tbsp oil/butter/bacon fat, plus ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper, ½ tsp garlic, ½ tsp paprika. Spread in an even layer and bake 20–25 min at 425°F until golden on top.
    ~20–25 min
  4. Cook bacon + sausage
    While potatoes crisp: cook bacon until crisp; chop. Brown sausage until no pink. Drain excess grease.
    ~15–18 min (overlaps step 3)
  5. Cook frozen carrots + peas DRY
    Add the frozen veg to the skillet (after meat is done). Cook until hot and the water is cooked off. If it looks wet, keep going.
    ~5–7 min
  6. Whisk egg mix
    Whisk 10 eggs with remaining seasoning: about 1 tsp salt (less if your bacon is salty), ½ tsp pepper, ½ tsp garlic, ½ tsp paprika, optional pinch cayenne.
    ~3–5 min
  7. Assemble
    Pull crisped hash browns. Scatter bacon + sausage + carrots/peas evenly. Optional: add cheese. Pour egg mixture over everything.
    ~5 min
  8. Final bake
    Reduce oven to 375°F. Bake 20–25 min, uncovered, until center is set (or 160°F).
    ~20–25 min
  9. Rest + serve
    Rest 10 min before slicing. This helps it set AND keeps the potato layer crisper.
    ~10 min

Crispiness Checklist

Squeeze the hash browns. If they can drip, they can sog.
Pre-crisp at 425°F. Crunch first, eggs later.
Cook carrots+peas dry. Veg moisture is stealth-sog.
Don’t cover while baking. Foil traps steam and turns crisp into “soft regret.”

Quick Reference

  • Oven: 425°F to pre-crisp, then 375°F to bake
  • Hash browns: thaw + squeeze + oil + bake 20–25 min
  • Veg: cook moisture off before adding
  • Doneness: center set, or 160°F
  • Rest: 10 min before slicing
Optional upgrades
• Add 1–1½ cups cheese for a golden lid
• Finish with a 2-minute broil (watch closely)
• Wider dish = more crispy edges
